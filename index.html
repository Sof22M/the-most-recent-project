How to Play

1. Use the arrow keys to move the star character (★) around the game area.

2. Collect the hiragana characters that spell the Japanese word equivalent to the English word shown above.

3. Correct characters will turn green, incorrect ones will turn red.

4. Complete all 10 words to finish the game!

5. You can upload your word sets to practice!
